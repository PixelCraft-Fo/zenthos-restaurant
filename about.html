Our Story

Who We Are

Zenthos was born from a passion for authentic Greek flavors. Nestled on the shores of Paralia Katerini, we bring you the soul of Greece — from hand-rolled dolmades to freshly grilled souvlaki, all enjoyed with a view of the Sea. Every dish is a journey. Every meal, a memory.
